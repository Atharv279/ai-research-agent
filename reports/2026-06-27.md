The project provides various features, including ask/planning/coding modes, in-place file editing, interactive terminal, and support for any LLM provider.

By bringing the model and code on-premises, Godcoder ensures data sovereignty and minimizes reliance on third-party services. This approach is notable for its potential to promote more decentralized and user-controlled AI development.
