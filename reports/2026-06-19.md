The project, initially created by Claude Code, enables users to synthesize and maintain a persistent wiki using the Karpathy pattern. Unlike traditional RAG (Recurrent Active Gatherer), which re-researches each question, this template accumulates updates from LLM directly, making it a unique approach to knowledge management.

**2. Key innovations**

One notable aspect of `llm-wiki` is its use of permanent markdown wiki template, allowing users to create a persistent and up-to-date knowledge base using an LLM. This contrasts with traditional approaches that re-research questions or rely on manual updates. Another innovation lies in the 4-layer structure: inbox (waiting for compilation), raw (unchanged original source), wiki (compiled results), and schema (operational conventions). This modular design enables users to easily maintain and update their knowledge base.
