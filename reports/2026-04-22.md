### Summary

**1. What it does**

QuantClaw is a plug-and-play task-type routing quantization plugin for OpenClaw, allowing users to balance quality, latency, and cost without manually choosing precision levels for their models. It classifies incoming requests into three precision tiers (4bit, 8bit, or 16bit) and routes them to the most suitable model target.

**2. Key innovations**

QuantClaw stands out due to its data-driven approach, built on quantization studies of OpenClaw workloads rather than fixed intuition. This ensures that the plugin is optimized for a wide range of tasks and models, making it a notable innovation in task-type routing and quantization.

**3. Tech stack**

* Primary Language: TypeScript
* Secondary Languages: Python (12.4%), Node.js (npm)

This plugin could also serve as a standard for task-type routing and quantization, promoting interoperability between different frameworks and libraries.
