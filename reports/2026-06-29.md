The praxis-cloak project aims to develop an on-device solution that intercepts and scrubbs Personal Identifiable Information (PII) from prompts sent to cloud Large Language Models (LLMs). This is achieved through a local model that swaps PII with realistic stand-ins, sends the scrubbed prompt to the cloud, and then maps the answer back to the original entities.
